TMB Guide

A Spectacular 170km Trek

The TMB (Tour du Mont Blanc) is a spectacular 170km trek through France, Italy, and Switzerland, circling the Mont Blanc massif. Planning accommodation along this iconic trail can be challenging, with dozens of refuges and varying availability throughout the season.

PlanTMB was created to simplify this process, helping hikers discover, compare, and book mountain huts with ease. Whether you're planning a complete circuit or shorter sections, our platform provides all the information you need to make your TMB adventure unforgettable.
